Highlights

- Rust library with 127 terrain, hydrology, and imagery algorithms
- Compiles to native, WebAssembly, and Python from one codebase
- Open-source implementation of Florinsky's complete 14-curvature morphometric system
- 1.8x faster than GDAL for slope; 23x faster for flow accumulation vs GRASS GIS
- Numerical accuracy within 10^-5 degrees of analytical solutions
